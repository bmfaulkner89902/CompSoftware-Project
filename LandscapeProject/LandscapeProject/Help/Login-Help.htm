Customer Portal

When the customer tab is selected this gives you the ability to access the Cusotmer functions of the program. Selecting the guest button will give you access to the job portal, but you will need to create an account to request a job. Selecting the create button will allow the user to create an account. This enables you to request jobs to be done. You can also recover an account that has alrady been created by clicking on the forgot username and passowrd link. Finally inputting your user information into the text boxes then clicking submit will give you acess to our job portal that is custom built based on your credentails. You will not be able to log in with incorrect information.

Employee Portal

When the employee tab is selected the user has the ability to login as an employee given they have the correct credentials. Input your username and password into the text field and click submit.

Owner Portal

When the owner tab is selected the user has the ability to login as an owner given they have the correct credentials. Input your username and password into the text field and click submit.
