Requesting a Job

To request a job fill in a job description i.e. Lawn care, Trim hedges, etc. Then give us the address yoiu would like the job to be done at. Please give us your full adress including state and zip code. Next select the date we can begin and then in the ending date field select the date the job needs to be done by. After all this click request job. This will put the job on your account and our Landscaperz employees will handle the rest.

Viewing Job Information

After a jobs been requested it will propagate in your job information portal when viewing job details its very important to click on the row header at the left of the job table. Here you can find the information you have requested about the job. When the job has been started you will see your Landscaperz employee info in the bottom viewer. This enables the user to contact our employees with any questions, commnets, or concerns. When the job is complete you will then see a payment due and can be paid for. After that is all completed our employee will take the job off your list.
