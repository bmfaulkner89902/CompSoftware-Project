Add A New Customer

In the First Box, Add A New Customer, there will be six TextBoxes that will allow you to enter the necessary information needed to add a new customer to the database. Once you have your information entered, there will be two buttons below the TextBoxes. If you click "Add Customer", it will add your information to the database and create that customer. If you click "Cancel Adding Customer", it will clear all the information that you had entered.
