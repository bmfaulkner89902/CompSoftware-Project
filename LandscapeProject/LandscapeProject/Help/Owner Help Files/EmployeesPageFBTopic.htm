Add A New Employee

In the First Box, Add A New Employee, there will be six TextBoxes that will allow you to enter the necessary information needed to add a new Employee to the database. Once you have your information entered, there will be two buttons below the TextBoxes. If you click "Add Employee", it will add your information to the database and create that employee. If you click "Cancel Adding Employee", it will clear all the information that you had entered.
