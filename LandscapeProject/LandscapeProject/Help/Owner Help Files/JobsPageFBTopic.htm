First Box With Material And Job Pages

This page will go in-depth to the functionality of everything on each page in the First Box.

- Functionality -

Job Materials

On the first page, Job Materials, there will be another Data Grid Viewer that will show information for all the materials that are associated with the job that was selected. Below the Viewer, there is a ComboBox that you can use to type or select a category (which is a column from the Viewer), and proceed to the TextBox below it to enter new information. When new information has been entered, click the "Submit Changes" button to update the database with the new information. If you click "Cancel Changes", it will clear your selection and entered information. If you click "Delete Selected Material", you will remove that material from its associated job.

Add Material

On the second page, Add Material, there will be four TextBoxes that will allow you to enter the necessary information needed to add a new material to the job you have selected. Once you have your information entered, there will be two buttons below the TextBoxes. If you click "Add Material", it will add your information to the database and associate that material to the job. If you click "Cancel Adding Material", it will clear all the information that you had entered.

Add Job

On the third page, Add Job, there will be six TextBoxes that will allow you to enter the necessary information needed to add a new job. Once you have your information entered, there will be two buttons below the TextBoxes. Note that you do not have to enter information for End Date or Price right away, you can add it later. If you click "Add Job", it will add your information to the database. If you click "Cancel Adding Job", it will clear all the information that you had entered.

Edit Job Info

On the fourth page, Edit Job Info, there will be a ComboBox that you can use to type or select a category (which is a column from the Viewer), and proceed to the TextBox below it to enter new information. When new information has been entered, click the "Submit Changes" button to update the database with the new information. If you click "Cancel Changes", it will clear your selection and entered information. If you click "Delete Selected Job", you will remove that Job as well as any materials associated with it and remove any workers that was working on that job.
